PB and J Recipe

Description

Here's a fantastic recipe for a childhood classic that will
make your mouth water!

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • 2 slices of bread
  • Grape Jelly

Steps

  1. Place the two pieces of bread on a plate
  2. Grab a butter knife and spread the peanut butter on one of the slices of bread
  3. Use the butter knife to then spread the jelly on the other slice of bread
  4. Put both pieces together and you have yourself a delicious PB&J!
